What the app does

Create and maintain a complete Amiibo inventory: mark figures you own and filter by series right inside your own spreadsheet. The app only adds and updates rows you choose, making it easy to keep everything organized in one place.

Why Amiibo Tracker asks for Google access

Google authentication is used to let you pick the specific Sheet you want to sync. Amiibo Tracker requests permission to view and edit that Sheet so the app can populate and update your collection data. The Amiibo Tracker app does not read emails or any other Drive files, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.
