About Amiibo Tracker

Amiibo Tracker is a tool for Nintendo Amiibo collectors to manage their collections, learn about NFC technology, and browse the complete catalog of released figures.

What Goozamiibo Offers

  • Collection Tracking — Maintain a complete Amiibo inventory in your own Google Sheet. Mark what you own, filter by series and type, and keep everything organized.
  • Favorites — Heart the figures you love most from your collection or the AmiiboDex, then filter to show only your favorites. Favorites are saved right alongside your collection in your Google Sheet.
  • AmiiboDex Catalog — Browse an always-updated database of every released Amiibo figure, with high-quality images, release dates, and series information. The catalog scrapes the web daily to add newly announced figures.
  • Educational Blog — Reviews, hunts, and guides covering NFC technology, Amiibo history, rarity tiers, condition grading, game compatibility, spotting fakes, and collecting tips.
  • Comments — Share your thoughts on individual figures and blog posts, reply to other collectors, and join the conversation.
  • Privacy-First Design — Your collection data stays in your own Google Drive. No shared databases, no tracking beyond basic analytics.
  • Dark Mode — Easy on the eyes with dark mode support that remembers your preference.
  • Mobile Friendly — Fully responsive design that works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

How It Works

  1. Sign in with Google. Goozamiibo uses Google sign-in to securely connect to your Google Sheets — no separate account or password to manage.
  2. Your collection sheet is set up automatically. On first sign-in, Goozamiibo creates a private AmiiboCollection sheet in your Google Drive (and reuses it on every visit after that).
  3. Track and favorite. Browse the catalog, mark figures as collected, and heart your favorites — every change saves straight to your Google Sheet.
  4. Stay organized. Search and filter by series, type, collected status, or favorites, and watch your progress with real-time collection counts.

Prefer to look before signing in? The interactive demo lets you try collecting and favoriting with sample data — nothing is saved.

Why Google Sheets?

Goozamiibo uses Google Sheets because it gives you complete control over your data. You can:

  • View and edit your collection directly in Google Sheets anytime
  • Export your data in any format (CSV, Excel, PDF)
  • Share your collection with friends or fellow collectors
  • Keep offline backups automatically through Google Drive
  • Revoke app access anytime from your Google account settings

Privacy & Permissions

Amiibo Tracker requests minimal permissions:

  • View and manage Google Sheets: Required to read and update your Amiibo collection data
  • See your email address: Used only to identify your Google account

Goozamiibo does NOT access your emails, other Drive files, or any personal information beyond what's necessary to manage your collection. Review the Privacy Policy for complete details.

Open Source & Free

Amiibo Tracker is free to use with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or subscriptions. The project is built with transparency in mind to help the Amiibo collecting community.
