About Amiibo Tracker

Amiibo Tracker is a tool for Nintendo Amiibo collectors to manage their collections, learn about NFC technology, and browse the complete catalog of released figures.

What Goozamiibo Offers

  • Collection Tracking — Maintain a complete Amiibo inventory in your own Google Sheet. Mark what you own, filter by series and type, and keep everything organized.
  • Complete Catalog — Browse an always-updated database of all released Amiibo figures with high-quality images, release dates, and series information.
  • Educational Blog — Learn about NFC technology, Amiibo history, pronunciation guides, compatibility with games, and collecting tips.
  • Privacy-First Design — Your data stays in your Google Drive. No shared databases, no tracking beyond basic analytics.
  • Dark Mode — Easy on the eyes with automatic dark mode support across the entire app.
  • Mobile Friendly — Fully responsive design that works seamlessly on phones, tablets, and desktops.

How It Works

  1. Sign in with Google. Goozamiibo uses Google authentication to securely access your Google Sheets.
  2. Create or select a sheet. Choose an existing Google Sheet or let Goozamiibo create a new one for your collection.
  3. Start tracking. Browse the catalog, mark Amiibo as collected, and your Google Sheet updates automatically.
  4. Stay organized. Filter by series, search by name or date, and track your progress with real-time collection counts.

Why Google Sheets?

Goozamiibo uses Google Sheets because it gives you complete control over your data. You can:

  • View and edit your collection directly in Google Sheets anytime
  • Export your data in any format (CSV, Excel, PDF)
  • Share your collection with friends or fellow collectors
  • Keep offline backups automatically through Google Drive
  • Revoke app access anytime from your Google account settings

Privacy & Permissions

Amiibo Tracker requests minimal permissions:

  • View and manage Google Sheets: Required to read and update your Amiibo collection data
  • See your email address: Used only to identify your Google account

Goozamiibo does NOT access your emails, other Drive files, or any personal information beyond what's necessary to manage your collection. Review the Privacy Policy for complete details.

Open Source & Free

Amiibo Tracker is free to use with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or subscriptions. The project is built with transparency in mind to help the Amiibo collecting community.
