How Google Sheets access works

When you sign in, Amiibo Tracker creates a Google Sheet named "AmiiboCollection" for you and only reads and writes to that file. We store your collected Amiibos, toggles, and preferences in that sheet so you keep control of your data and can revoke access at any time. No other documents or Google Drive files are touched.

Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties. Access is strictly limited to the functions you grant through Google.
