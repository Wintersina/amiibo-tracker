Privacy Policy

Amiibo Tracker relies on Google Sign-In only to authenticate you and to connect to your personal Google Sheet named "AmiiboCollection." We do not use a separate database; all of your collection data lives in your own Google Sheet so you stay in control.

Data Usage

We use your Google profile (email and basic account name) to keep your session tied to the correct Google account and to show which profile is signed in. Google Sheets access is used strictly to create, read, and update the "AmiiboCollection" sheet that stores your collection status, toggles, and preferences. We do not access any other Google Drive files or services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties. The only external service involved is Google Sheets, which is used exclusively to store the data you create inside your own Google account.

Data Storage & Protection

Amiibo Tracker does not maintain a separate database or server-side store for your information. All collection data is written to your personal "AmiiboCollection" Google Sheet. Access to that sheet is protected by your Google account permissions, and you can revoke our access at any time through your Google account settings.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains in your Google Sheet until you delete it. If you remove our access or delete the "AmiiboCollection" sheet, we can no longer read or write your data. If you need help removing data, contact us and we will guide you through deleting the sheet or revoking access.
