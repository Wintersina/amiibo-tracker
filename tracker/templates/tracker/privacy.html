Privacy Policy

Amiibo Tracker relies on Google Sign-In only to authenticate you and to connect to your personal Google Sheet named "AmiiboCollection." The app does not use a separate database or server-side store; all of your collection data stays in your own Google Sheet so you stay in control.

Data Usage

Amiibo Tracker uses your Google profile (email and basic account name) to keep your session tied to the correct Google account and to show which profile is signed in. Google Sheets access is used strictly to create, read, and update the "AmiiboCollection" sheet that stores your collection status, toggles, and preferences. No other Google Drive files or services are accessed, and your data is not used for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

  • Access to Google data is limited to the scopes required to sign you in and manage the "AmiiboCollection" sheet.
  • Data is processed only to provide Amiibo Tracker features (syncing your collection and preferences).
  • The application follows the Google API Services User Data Policy and Google APIs Terms of Service when requesting and using your information.

Data Sharing

Amiibo Tracker does not sell, share, or distribute your data to any third parties. The only external services involved are Google Sign-In (for authentication) and Google Sheets (for storage). No data is shared with advertising networks or analytics providers.

Data Storage & Protection

Amiibo Tracker does not maintain a separate database or server-side store for your information. All collection data is written to your personal "AmiiboCollection" Google Sheet. Access to that sheet is protected by your Google account permissions, and you can revoke app access at any time through your Google account settings.

  • No Amiibo data is persisted on Goozamiibo's servers; any data processed during a session remains in memory only long enough to complete your request.
  • All connections to Google APIs use HTTPS to protect data in transit.
  • The app does not log or export the contents of your sheet outside your Google account.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains in your Google Sheet until you delete it. If you remove app access or delete the "AmiiboCollection" sheet, Amiibo Tracker can no longer read or write your data. You control removal by deleting the sheet or revoking access yourself.

  • To delete all stored information, remove or clear the "AmiiboCollection" sheet in your Google Drive.
  • To revoke Goozamiibo's access, visit https://myaccount.google.com/permissions and remove Amiibo Tracker.

For privacy questions, email codingsina@gmail.com. Deleting your data remains under your control via your Google account.
